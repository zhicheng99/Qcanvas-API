##### 创建二次贝塞尔曲线quadraticCurve对象

```
this.qcanvas.qquadraticCurve.quadraticCurve({
		start:[20,20],
		handler:[100,200],
		end:[100,20],
		width:1,
		like:'<-->',
		color:'red',
		drag:true,
		withText:'fsfds'
	})
```
